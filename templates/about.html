About Maria

A prolific writer of short fiction, articles, essays, and poetry, Maria's work has been published in or is forthcoming from {% for place in publisher_list %} {% if not forloop.last %}{{place}}, {% else %} and {{place}}. {% endif %} {% endfor %}

In her previous lives, Maria's held a variety of diverse careers, including property manager, English teacher, and occupational therapist; she's currently a computer programmer. She lives with her partner a potato-dog in southwest Michigan, in a centenarian saltbox house that is almost certainly haunted. She loves a good K-Drama and Bell's beer.

She is represented by Amy Bishop at Dystel, Goderich & Bourret. She can also be reached via twitter @mariadongwrites or via email with the contact form. If you'd like to be notified when new fiction is published, you can also sign up for the newsletter.

About Maria

Maria Dong's short fiction has been published in a variety of magazines, including Augur, Apparition Lit, and Fusion Fragment, among others. Her Korean mythology inspired fantasy novel, BAD MOON CHILD, was featured in the 2018 Pitch Wars showcase.
She is represented by Amy Bishop at Dystel, Goderich & Bourret. She can also be reached via twitter @mariadongwrites or via email at [maria at mariadong.com].
